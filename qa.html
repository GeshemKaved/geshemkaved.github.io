Why should i use LoRa style device when i have my smartphone? (for calls and internet)

LoRa (Long Range) is a low-power wide-area network and is used where traditional GSM and WIFI fails. For Example in the middle of the desart, or after a catastrophe or where u get no GSM reception for any reason.

Why use Mesh network?

We use mesh in order to extend the distanst between the fartest points of nodes, and provide better connectivity overall. (A mesh network is a local network topology in which the infrastructure nodes connect directly, dynamically and non-hierarchically to as many other nodes as possible and cooperate with one another to efficiently route data from/to clients.)

What is the maximum range between 2 points.

This depends on the nu,ber of active users, and their location. From the ranges we have tested a 30Km between 2 points directly. (we did not try more then that, internet user report up to 70Km.
